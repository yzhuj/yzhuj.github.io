  • Convert between W, dBm, Vpp, and Vrms
    • This script converts between:
    • W: 1 Watt = 30 dBm = 20 Vpp = 7.07 Vrms
    • dBm: dB relative to 1 mW. ± 10 dBm = order of magnitude change in Watts; ± 3 dBm ~ factor of 2 change in Watts
    • Vpp: Volts peak-to-peak of a sine wave
    • Vrms: Root mean square voltage (effective DC voltage with the same power as the AC signal)

  • Fiber Coupling
    • This script computes the focal lengh of the lens required to mode-match a free-space Gaussian beam with an optical fiber. The focal length is given by f = π D⋅MFD/4λ. The optical fiber has fixed NA; therefore, a larger beam requires a longer focal length lens compared to a smaller beam. In the ray picture, the diameter of the collimated beam is D = 2f⋅NA where NA is the numerical aperture of the fiber.
