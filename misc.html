  • Laser Intensity

  • dBm to W
    • This script converts power in dBm (dB wrt 1 mW) to Watts. The conversion is given by 10^(dBm/10 - 3). Therefore, +/- 10 dBm corresponds to an order-of-magnitude of power in Watts and +/- 3 dBm corresponds to a factor of 2 of power in Watts.
    —
  • W to dBm
    • This script converts power in Watts to dBm. The conversion is given by 10 [log10(Watts) + 3]. Therefore, order-of-magnitude of power in Watts corresponds to +/- 10 dBm and a factor of 2 of power in Watts corresponds to +/- 3 dBm .
    —
  • Gaussian Beam Propagation
    • This script computes the Rayleigh range and beam waist of a Gaussian beam.

  • Fiber Coupling
    • This script computes the focal lengh of the lens required to mode-match a free-space Gaussian beam with an optical fiber. The focal length is given by f = π D⋅MFD/4λ. The optical fiber has fixed NA; therefore, a larger beam requires a longer focal length lens compared to a smaller beam.
