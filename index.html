Yi Zhu

My name is Yi. In fall 2022 I will start my PhD at Harvard University studying Quantum Science and Engineering.

I completed my undergraduate degree in Engineering Physics at the University of California, Berkeley where I explored quantum information with trapped ions in the Häffner Group. At Berkeley, I also served for 3 years as an instructor, 2 of which as director, of the Undergraduate Lab at Berkeley, Physics & Astronomy Division. ULAB is a course that facilitates underpresented and undersupported undergradutes taking their first steps into research.
